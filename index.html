Our school's water guide

Hello everybody, during this project we learned about: saving water, keeping our
water sources clean, why we need water and the importance of water.
Saving water is crucial for our society because water is life and without water
we can't fulfill our daily needs.
Conserving water
We learned that we need to practice conserving water so anytime we have any
water shortages, we can use the conserved water to fulfill our needs. We
learned that keeping water clean is super important for our school and also
our community. We learned that we should keep our schools water sources
clean so we can have healthy clean water. We often use the saying “leave the
bathroom how you wish to find it” so it can remind us of how important clean
water is. We hope you like our project as much as we did!

Students' opinion about our work:

Designer

Blina

I would happily finish this project off by saying how only a little bit of change to your
water usage routine can do so much. Even the tiniest amount of water could mean
so much for a lot of people, because
not all environments have open access to water. Instead of taking longer showers,
leaving the faucet on, pouring or spilling
water without an actual reason, do the opposite
and start to conserve water and take care of
your lakes, rivers, creeks, ponds, swamps and ocean from of which thousands of
people obtain free water from. This is very important for them because humans need
water to survive and the healthy amount of water you need to drink in a day is 3.7
liters but not everyone can afford 15.5 cups of safe and fresh water per day. My point
is that all these little differences can make so many changes, so i recommend you
start to be more water wise, too.

Project Manager

Amela
